Day Tour

  • Entrance Fee: Php 50 entrance (Free for 12 yrs old under)
  • Day Tour Rates: Php 350 per table or shed

Highlights:

Enjoy the property for the day. We offer cabanas, sheds, tables & chairs, benches, toilets, barbeque, kitchen. You can also enjoy swimming, snorkeling to the coral reef right at the property.

Tent Camping Php 500 per night

Experience the camping right on the property. You can bring your own tent and enjoy the sundown and sun rise. You can collect some wood in the area and make your own fire to cook or bonfire in the evening. We have kitchen , toilet and shower provided as well. You can walk form our property to the long beach.
