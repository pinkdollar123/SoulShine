Soul Shine Beach House is located in Sitio Talaudyong, Barangay Bacungan, Puerto Princesa, Palawan. This vacation rental house is just waiting for you to enjoy your vacation. The House is set right on the beach, just steps from the water. You will experience panoramic stunning views of the ocean and beach. Barbeque, Swim, Snorkel to the coral reef right from the property.

Soul Shine Beach House is the perfect takeoff point for your ultimate Palawan experience. We offer several choices of accommodation tailor fit for your dream vacation.
